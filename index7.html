The Farm

About

"The Farm" is a bone-chilling horror film that takes you on a terrifying journey. Set in a remote countryside farm, the movie delves into the dark secrets that lie within. When a young couple decides to spend a weekend getaway at the farm, they unknowingly step into a nightmare. As night falls, they encounter unexplained phenomena, eerie noises, and malevolent forces that torment their every move. With each passing moment, their sanity is tested, and their lives are in grave danger. "The Farm" is a gripping and suspenseful film that will keep you on the edge of your seat, questioning what lurks in the shadows.

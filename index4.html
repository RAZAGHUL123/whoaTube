About

"Terrifier" is a chilling horror film that revolves around a mysterious and malevolent clown. On a fateful Halloween night, two young women find themselves trapped and pursued by the sadistic clown in an abandoned building. With its suspenseful atmosphere and gruesome sequences, "Terrifier" delivers a nerve-wracking and disturbing tale that will leave audiences on the edge of their seats.

Lake Alice

About

"Lake Alice" is a suspenseful thriller that takes place in a remote lakeside town. The film follows a group of friends who gather at a secluded cabin by the tranquil Lake Alice. As the night unfolds, they find themselves entangled in a series of mysterious and unsettling events that test their bonds and push them to their limits. With its atmospheric setting and suspenseful storytelling, "Lake Alice" keeps viewers on the edge of their seats, unraveling secrets and surprises until the gripping finale.
